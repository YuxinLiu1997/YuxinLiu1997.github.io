    Jiameng Lyu

I am a second-year PhD student in Operations Research at the Department of Industrial Engineering, University of Pittsburgh. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Amin Rahimian. Prior to Pitt, I received my B.S. in Economics from Jilin University (June 2020) and my M.E. in System Engineering from Tianjin University (June 2023).

My research passion lies in the intersection of data-driven decision-making and operations management. Currently, I am focusing on two important directions:

  • Develop flexibly applicable, computationally efficient, and provably optimal data-driven decision-making frameworks that work for a wide range of problems.
  • Design fairness-aware data-driven decision-making methods for operations management scenarios.
Here is my CV. Feel free to contact me through Email (lvjm21@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn).

I am on the 2024-2025 academic job market (targeting positions in Asia).
